Honey-Mustard Glazed Salmon

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets
  • 2/3 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

Directions

  1. Step 1: Put the fillets on a greased baking sheet skin side down.
  2. Step 2: Mix the brown sugar, mustard, honey, and salt in a small bowl, spoon it over the salmon.
  3. Step 3: Broil the salmon 3-4 inches from the heat until it easily flakes with a fork, which takes about 8-12 minutes.
